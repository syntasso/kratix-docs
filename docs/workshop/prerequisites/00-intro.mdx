Before diving into the workshops, you will need to prepare your environment. This includes:

1. Installing the required software on your machine
1. Getting your hands on a couple of Kubernetes Clusters
1. Preparing one of the clusters to run Kratix
1. Preparing one of the clusters to run the Kratix Workloads

So head to the [next section](./setup) to get started!
