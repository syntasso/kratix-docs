:::warning Deprecated

The Backstage Controller and the `ske-backstage-generator` are deprecated in favour of the [Portal Controller](/ske/integrations/portal-controller/intro), which replaces them
(and the Cortex Controller) with a single, unified integration. They continue to work for at least one more release. See
[Migrating from the Backstage Controller](/ske/integrations/portal-controller/migrating-from-backstage) when you are ready to move.

:::
