### Pictures and diagrams

An image or diagram can both break up any walls of text and help users process the content on the page with greater speed and ease.

Interesting reading about the role of images in processing:
- [Pictorial illustrations still improve students' learning from
  text](https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Russell-Carney/publication/263116114_Pictorial_Illustrations_Still_Improve_Students'_Learning_from_Text/links/02e7e51754d7237561000000/Pictorial-Illustrations-Still-Improve-Students-Learning-from-Text.pdf)
- [Picture This: Studying the Effectiveness of Illustrated Instructions](https://ir.library.oregonstate.edu/concern/articles/8910k362j)

But when using images, they must be sure to be relevant to the context they're in, comprehensible to the intended audience and accessible.
