Released in Helm Chart version [v0.14.0](http://syntasso-enterprise-releases.s3-website.eu-west-2.amazonaws.com/#ske-gui-helm-chart/0.14.0/)

Includes a fix that stops the Resource list view from crashing when many Promises and Resources are created and deleted at the same time.

:::important
Please note that as of v0.6.3, the SKE GUI image will be accessible at ghcr.io not registry.syntasso.io.

Please ensure that your Syntasso Registry secret is updated to reflect this. If it is not updated, your cluster will be unable to pull
the image and the ske-gui pod will fail to start.
:::
